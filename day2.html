7 WONDERS OF WORLD

1.GREAT WALL OF CHINA

The Great Wall of China is a vast series of interconnected fortifications built over centuries to defend China's northern border from nomadic invaders. It is the world's longest man-made structure, with a total length of over 21,196 kilometers (13,170 miles), and consists of walls, watchtowers, and fortresses. Built using materials like rammed earth, stone, bricks, and mortar, its most well-known sections were constructed during the Ming Dynasty (1368-1644).

2.MACHU PICCHHUU

Machu Picchu is considered a wonder due to the advanced engineering of its Incan builders, the site's harmonious integration with its dramatic natural setting, its status as a unique and remarkably preserved testament to the Inca civilization, and its deep cultural, historical, and spiritual significance

3.TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal was designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1983 for being "the jewel of Islamic art in India and one of the universally admired masterpieces of the world's heritage".
